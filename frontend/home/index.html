Thanks for dropping by our website!

We currently have many adoptable dogs available looking for forever homes. We strive to place each dog in a home that fits his or her individual needs.

We are always looking for foster homes and donations to keep our rescue going! We rely on the kindness of other animal lovers to be able to continue and save as many lives as possible but we can't do it alone! Your help is greatly appreciated!

We wish to offer services as a way to help fundraise: pet sitting, dog walking, and light grooming.

Help Make a Difference in Their Lives

Every animal deserves a chance at a happy, healthy life. We are dedicated to providing just that, but we can't do it alone. Any donations would be vital in helping us sustain the care and love these animals need.

Where Your Contribution Goes

When you donate to Patties Angels, you are directly impacting the lives of animals in need. Your generosity will help us cover essential costs such as:

  • Food: To keep our furry friends well-fed and energetic.
  • Cleaning Supplies: To maintain a clean and hygienic environment for them to thrive in.
  • Medical Expenses: Including veterinary bills for regular check-ups and any necessary treatments or surgeries.

No contribution is too small, and every bit helps us make a significant difference. With your support, we can continue to provide a sanctuary where love and care know no bounds.
